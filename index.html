Crystals Collector

How to Play

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Win the game by matching your total score to the random, target score. You lose the game if your total score goes above the target score.

Determine the value of each crystal by clicking on it. Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
